VisBAR_wave _batch[*]  is a Python-based visualization tool for electronic wavefunctions. 

VisBAR_wave_batch is a part of VisBAR package (VisBAR=Visualization tool with Ball, Arrow , Rod)[*][1]. 

If you use VisBAR_wave_batch in a publication, please cite the paper [1].

Details are explained in the japanase manual.
An english manual is in preparation.  

[*] http://www.damp.tottori-u.ac.jp/~hoshi/visbar/

[1] Takeo Hoshi, Yohei Akiyama, Tatsunori Tanaka and Takahisa Ohno, 
"Ten-million-atom electronic structure calculations on the K computer with a massively parallel order-N theory",
J. Phys. Soc. Jpn. 82, 023710, 4pp (2013).
http://dx.doi.org/10.7566/JPSJ.82.023710
